A behavior change poster assignment

About this project

This was our first project of the module, we had to learn how to use Adobe Illustrator to create a poster that effectively communicates a behavior change message.

I have worked with Adobe programs before but I have never worked with Illustrator so that was a nice challenge for me.

I gotta be honest, I would rather use Photoshop for this kind of work. Since it's way easier and more intuitive for me.

For this project I was trying to make a poster about the effects of a gaming addiction.

The effects that this poster tries to communicate is an addiction to gaming and energy drinks to compensate for lack of sleep.

Some would suggest the message is "Choose your game time wisely." and others would not agree.
